About Us

After the Irish Famine, much of the citizens were displaced.


However, our family, the O' Dooley clan, never left.


We stayed and cleaned for the landlords, holding firm the belief we would one day have our Land reclaimed and have a strong and much needed business to grow and prosper from.


So, Mr. and Mrs. O' Dooley set-about the strong foundation our business was born from. Ever since then, we have only gotten stronger and better at the craft of cleaning!


So you can trust us, with over 169 years of family history and skills behind us, to complete whatever cleaning task is placed in front of us!
